### Travel Group PM

Travel Group PM is a portfolio project focused on validating whether collaborative trip planning can reduce coordination issues and increase participation in group travel organization.

### Problem

In many group trips, most of the planning work falls on a single person. This often leads to:

* Fragmented information across chats and apps.

* Unclear task ownership.

* Low participation from other group members.

* Difficulty tracking progress.

### Project Objective

Validate whether greater visibility of tasks, planning progress, and shared travel documents can improve collaboration during group trip organization.

### Current Phase

Discovery & MVP Definition

### Initial Hypothesis

If participants can clearly see pending tasks, overall progress, and shared travel information in one place, they will be more likely to contribute to the organization process.
